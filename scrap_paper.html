The City Market is a community-driven public market that sets up twice a week from the beginning of April to the end of October.

Hours and Address

Thursday 4 PM to 8 PM

Saturday 8 AM to 2 PM

321 E. Commercial Street

Springfield, MO, 65803

Amy and the vendors are being diligent about protecting the wellbeing of our patrons. We are all taking the utmost care to protect ourselves and everyone who comes to see us. If you need it, feel free to ask at the manager's booth for a squirt of sanitizer.

Our People

Amy Truitt manages the market with grace and aplomb. She started her career as a vendor before becoming the master of ceremonies and petter of puppies. Amy is a familiar face around Commercial Street, since she also manages Chabom Tea and Spice.

The heart of the market is the fabulous vendors who make delicious and fresh produce, succulent baked goods, and ingenious crafts.

Chris and John Miller met in the army and have been buying and selling produce for the last (fact check) years. They have a boistrous young German Shepherd who will beat you with her tail.

Julia Sweeny has been roasting coffee beans for over 20 years and has been with the Market for (fact check) years.

Patrick and Anjulie Bowen do many interesting things with tiny bits of metal and paper.

Many good dogs frequent the market each week. If you see one coming up to you, be sure to ask their human if it's okay to pet them.
