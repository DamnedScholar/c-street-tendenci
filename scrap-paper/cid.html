Mission

The CID (Community Improvement District) is a local governmental subdivision put in place by voters in the federally recognized Historic Commercial Street district. It was initially founded in 2005 for a period of 15 years and renewed in 2019. The CID is governed by a board of directors and run in a manner similar to a not-for-profit entity, with operating revenues principally derived from a percentage of sales taxes paid in the district. Projects funded by the CID include, but aren't limited to, the landscaping of the street and the maintenance of this site.

CID meetings take place at the Savoy Ballroom on the second Wednesday of each month. The meeting runs from 10:00 - 11:00.

Getting Involved

If you're interested in learning more about what the CID does, the best way is to show up to a meeting! If you would like to speak at a meeting, you should send a message to CID@HistoricCStreet.com to request that the President put you on the agenda. If possible, please give notice by the Monday (two days) before the meeting.
